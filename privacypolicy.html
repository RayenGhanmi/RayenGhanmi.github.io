Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use my open-source projects and website. I am committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. I do not collect or process any analytics data. The only data collected is information you voluntarily provide when contacting me directly.

Information Collection

I do not collect any personal information unless you choose to share it by contacting me. Below is the type of data I may collect:

  • Personal identification information (e.g., name, email address) when you reach out to me for support, inquiries, or feedback.
  • Non-personal data such as general performance metrics and usage statistics may be collected through the use of my website and projects. However, this data is anonymized, and it is not linked to any individual user.

Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries: To provide assistance and address any support requests or questions you may have.

Open-Source Services

All features in my projects are based on open-source services and tools. I do not rely on any third-party services that may collect or process data. The software used in my projects adheres to open-source principles, and any external dependencies are also open-source. As such, you can review the source code and privacy practices of any components used directly from their respective repositories.

Your Rights

  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of any personal data I hold, where applicable, except in cases where I am required to retain it for legal or operational purposes.
