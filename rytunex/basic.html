Basic Optimizations

Basic Optimizations Section Allows You To Control The Following:

Performance Tweaks:

Enhance your system's performance by toggling various tweaks tailored to optimize its functionality.

Learn more about what's being disabled

Disable Superfetch:

Improve your system's startup speed by deactivating Superfetch, which preloads frequently used applications.

Disable NTFS Timestamp:

Optimize file operations by turning off NTFS timestamp updates.

Enable Gaming Mode:

Elevate your gaming experience with enhanced performance by enabling Gaming Mode.

Exclude Drivers From Windows Updates:

Take control of your driver updates by excluding them from the Windows Update process.
